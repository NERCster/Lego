Lego For Sale

Update: Two thirds of the Lego has been sold, so we've reduced the prices of most of the remaining items.

My son is selling most of his huge Lego collection in order to fund driving lessons and put money towards a car (they grow up so quick!). All these sets have been only lightly played with and very well looked after, in a smoke-free home. All sets from the past 15 years were bought new.

Given the large number of sets, it hasn't been possible to go through them all to guarantee every set is 100% complete. Having said that, he's always been painstakingly careful with his Lego, and so I'm sure most sets are complete to the best of our knowledge (unless we state otherwise). In the event a set is described as complete and turns out to have missing part(s), we'll do our best to track down the missing pieces from the Lego he's retaining.

Where a set comes with the original box, some of these have been carefully flattened for storage - the boxes might require a little glue to reconstruct (although some just slot together). Everything is in excellent condition, unless stated otherwise. I've included the Lego set numbers, so you can look them up on a site such as Brickset.

If you want to buy anything or have any questions, please contact me at sean.terry@ukri.org. If you can help me by quoting the #XX number of any item(s) you are interested in, that would be great.

I can bring items into Polaris House, for those in the Research Councils, or they can be picked up from our home in West Swindon for those on Gumtree.
